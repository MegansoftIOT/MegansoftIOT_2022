MeganSoft IoT Healthcare Solutions and Applications

The MeganSoft IoT Platform brings advanced Medical IoT solutions into the mainstream

Medical IoT spurred a new generation of devices that have now become lifelinesfor thousands of people. We are glad to contribute to this Healthcare transformation with MeganSoft.

The Healthcare industry has been one of the quickest to adopt the Internet of Things. The reason for this trend is that integrating IoT features into medical devices greatly improves the quality and effectiveness of service, bringing especially high value for the elderly, patients with chronic conditions, and those requiring constant supervision. According to some estimates, some estimates, spending on Healthcare IoT solutions will reach a staggering $1 trillion by 2025 and, hopefully, will set the stage for highly personalized, accessible, and on-time healthcare services for everyone.

MeganSoft, as an end-to-end enterprise IoT platform, allows medical OEMs and healthcare system integrators to establish cross-device connectivity and to implement smart features into medical devices and related software systems.

As a future-proof IoT technology that keeps up with the new challenges faced by the global Healthcare industry, the MeganSoft IoT Platform makes a specific emphasis on the cohesive integration of IoT, cloud, and Big Data innovations. Smart medical devices are tremendous in and of themselves, but it is no less important to provide healthcare providers with the powers to orchestrate this new technology, derive more value from data analytics, digitize their data transfer workflows, and connect with other parties in their healthcare ecosystem. That’s why our platform offers a variety of customization options via a purpose-built UI, open IoT protocols for third-party integrations, and microservice-based architecture, which is compatible with modern cloud applications and services. As a medical device manufacturer, you can distribute MeganSoft inside your products to enable some useful IoT features, while as a healthcare service provider, you can use MeganSoft to create a centralized smart hospital solution that functions as a control hub for your medical devices, data stores, and other digital assets. From remote data monitoring to device management to analytics & alerts, you can set up your Healthcare IoT use case from A to Z with MeganSoft.
